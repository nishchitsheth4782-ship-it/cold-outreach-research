# Sources — Cold Outreach Pipeline for B2B SaaS

10 practitioners who run (or have personally run) cold outreach motions and
publish about it regularly. Verify each link/date before final submission —
handles, follower counts, and post frequency change.

Last verified: 2026-07-05

---

### 1. Josh Braun
- **Why he qualifies:** Independent sales trainer, ex-seller; built "Badass
  Growth Guide" specifically for cold email/DM openers. Posts weekly,
  breaking down real cold emails he receives (screenshots + rewrites).
- **LinkedIn:** https://www.linkedin.com/in/josh-braun
- **Format:** LinkedIn posts (primary), guest podcast appearances
- **Annotation:** Best source for message/copywriting-level tactics — subject
  lines, opening lines, CTA design. Less focused on tooling/infrastructure.

---

### 2. Alex Berman
- **Why he qualifies:** Ran cold email agencies (Experiment27, then
  Email10k); Amazon bestseller author on cold email. Practices via his own
  coaching cohorts and client work.
- **LinkedIn:** https://www.linkedin.com/in/alexanderberman
- **YouTube:** Search "Alex Berman cold email" — active channel with cold
  email and agency growth teardown videos, several per month.
- **Annotation:** Best source for the agency/list-building/offer side of
  outreach. Tests messaging live with paying clients so data is real.

### 3. Will Aitken
- **Why he qualifies:** Sales creator and trainer; regularly live-tests
  outreach tools and cadences and publishes cost/response-rate comparisons
  with real numbers, not vendor marketing claims.
- **LinkedIn:** https://www.linkedin.com/in/justwillaitken
- **Annotation:** Best source for tooling comparisons (data providers, AI
  writing tools) and what's actually moving response rates right now.

- **LinkedIn:** https://www.linkedin.com/in/morganjingramamp

- **Why he qualifies:** Founder of QuickMail (cold email platform); co-hosts

- **Podcast:** "Cold Email Outreach with Jeremy & Jack" — Apple Podcasts /

- **Why he qualifies:** Founder of SalesBread, a lead-gen agency running

- **Podcast:** "Cold Email Outreach with Jeremy & Jack" — Apple Podcasts /

- **Why he qualifies:** Built outbound functions at Looker and Clari from
