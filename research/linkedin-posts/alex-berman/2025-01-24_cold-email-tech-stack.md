My cold email tech stack (here's what works in 2025):

I’ve generated over $100 million with cold email - but do you know the biggest reason most people fail at it?

They’re trying to wing it with bad tools, outdated strategies, or no system at all.

The truth is, the old cold email methods that worked even a year ago are dead.

Google and Outlook have cracked down...

Sending 30 emails per domain through Google Workspace is now a direct ticket to the spam folder.

You need a better system.

Here’s the stack I’m using in 2025 to stay ahead and ensure my emails hit inboxes, connect with the right people, and drive results:

1. Build a Lead Pool

Use LinkedIn Sales Navigator, Apollo.io to build the generic list...

BuiltWith is a killer for targeting competitors - just search their software and download a lead list - that's my favorite if you have a famous competitor that you're better than

2. Verify Emails with Automation

Clay is my go-to for setting up a “verification waterfall.”

It checks emails across multiple providers to save time and improve accuracy - Findymail, Datagma, LeadMagic... they're all in there

For advanced users, you can use APIs instead of Clay credits to cut costs

3. Personalize with AI

This approach is far more powerful than generic compliments - the old strategy we pioneered back in 2015.

Use AI to identify their competitors, highlight pain points, or even surface hidden fears.

Then your emails will read like a human wrote them

4. Send Campaigns with the Right Tools (Custom SMTP)

While there are a ton of vendors out there, the easiest way to get started is by joining the AB Mastermind and using our setup.

It helps you skip the large onboarding fees that other tools charge, saving you time and money (ours is only a couple hundred bucks)
We paid the large setup fees so you don't have to haha

All you have to do is remain an active mastermind member

5. Manage Warm Leads

Start with Google Sheets to track, follow up, and close deals

Warm call anybody that is interested as fast as possible (using Close)

and then keep calling everyday until you get through

But here’s the thing: knowing the tools isn’t enough.

Most people fail because they don’t know how to put it all together.

What email sequences work? How do you craft the perfect pitch? How do you avoid landing in spam?

That’s where I come in.

Inside the AB Mastermind, you’ll learn how to:

* Build a reliable cold email system tailored to your niche

* Land 5-10 new high-quality clients every month

* Avoid spam filters and stay ahead of Google and Outlook's updates

People inside the Mastermind are already landing deals worth $50k, $100k, even $1M using these strategies.

We've scaled agencies to $350k/month and way beyond - all with cold email.

Most likely there’s someone that's read my book, taken our courses or is actively inside the mastermind right now in your niche with your exact offer who’s booking a crazy amount of meetings. Are you next?
