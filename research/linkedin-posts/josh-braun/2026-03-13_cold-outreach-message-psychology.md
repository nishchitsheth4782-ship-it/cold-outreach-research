## Raw content

This 24 word cold outreach message made me respond. 

Yes you can steal it, but it’s the underlying psychology that will help you apply it your prospects.

The message:

“Josh, saw you’re doing Ironman Cozumel. I’m helping several Boca Tri athletes stay injury-free for that race. Ever worked with a Kinstretch coach?”

The Psychology:

“Josh, saw you’re racing Ironman Cozumel.”
Starts with relevance. Something familiar about me, not the sender. That grabs my attention and lowers resistance.

“Boca triathlon group.”
Creates connection through shared context. She’s not a stranger, she’s part of the same tribe. 

“Prevent injury”
Something I want to avoid.

“Ever worked with a Kinstretch coach before?”
Invites reflection instead of reaction. “Hmm, what’s that?”
It’s not leading or assumptive. 
It opens a conversation.
