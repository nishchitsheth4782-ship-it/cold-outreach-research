## Raw content

The best way to handle objections?

Is never to hear them. 

How?

Bring them up first. 

Examples: 

(Price)
“You’re probabaly wondering how much the workshop is.”

(This won’t work for us)
“You’re probably skeptical that a tool can solve your commission problems.”

(We have e vendor for that)
“I know you sell courses. When a credit card fails, how are you recovering those payments? Are you handling that yourself, or using an external team?”

It’s called elicitation.

You can’t plant an objection that’s not there. 

If you guess right people will confirm.
If you guess wrong they’ll correct.

Either way you unlock more truth.

Just because you don’t call out the elephants in the room doesn’t mean they don’t exist.
